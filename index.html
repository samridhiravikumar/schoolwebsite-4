what's new

  • Canteen/Hall

    St. Michael's now has a new canteen.It is as big as two class rooms and well organised, more efficient than they have been in the past. Online Menus with the photographs of the food that is prepared and the detailed description of the ingredients help the students and parents make the right nutritional choices.

    Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen would be provided with a School canteen account that keeps track of previous meal choices and any dietry requirements they may have.Ordering online by clicking on a box and paying money online can eliminate all the fuss of money handling,making it safe and less complicated for staff and students alike.Home again,delivery is the key.
